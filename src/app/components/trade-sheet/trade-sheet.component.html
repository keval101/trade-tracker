What is Trade Sheet?

Trade Sheet is a powerful tool to plan and track your trading goals over a specific period. It helps you set daily profit targets based on your starting capital and expected ROI, then track your actual performance against those targets.

How to Use:

  1. Generate a Sheet: Click "Generate Sheet" and enter your starting capital, expected ROI percentage, and number of days.
  2. Select Market: Choose your trading market (Nifty, Bank Nifty, Fin Nifty, etc.) to calculate point requirements.
  3. Add Daily Entries: Click "Add Entry" each day to record your actual profit or loss.
  4. Track Progress: The table shows your expected vs actual performance, helping you stay on track.
  5. Edit Entries: Click on any row in the table to edit that day's trade entry.

Understanding the Table:

  • Starting Capital: Your capital at the beginning of each day.
  • Current Capital: Your capital after previous day's trades.
  • Expected Profit: The daily profit target based on your ROI.
  • Point Needed: How many points you need to move in the selected market to achieve expected profit.
  • Current Profit: Your actual profit/loss for that day.
  • ROI: Return on investment percentage for that day.
  • Net: Final capital after adding/subtracting profit/loss.

Tip: A sheet is marked as "Target Achieved" when you complete all days and your final capital meets or exceeds the expected final capital.
